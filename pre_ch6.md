## 1. Letter

### K  /  k
형태: K (uppercase) — k (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② diagonal up-right from the middle → ③ diagonal down-right from the same point
트레이싱: Trace the big K and the small k. Start at the top.

### L  /  l
형태: L (uppercase) — l (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② horizontal line right at the bottom
트레이싱: Trace the big L and the small l. Start at the top.

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /k/ (K)
> Tongue goes to the back of your mouth. Push the air out — /k/ /k/ /k/!

Sight Words: we / he

Words:
  - kite 🪁
  - key 🔑
  - king 👑

Sound: /l/ (L)
> Touch the top of your mouth with your tongue tip. Say /l/ /l/ /l/!

Words:
  - lion 🦁
  - lemon 🍋
  - leaf 🍃

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /k/ → Students: /k/
  Teacher: "kite" → Students: "kite"
  Teacher: "key" → Students: "key"
  Teacher: /l/ → Students: /l/
  Teacher: "lion" → Students: "lion"
  Teacher: "lemon" → Students: "lemon"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "We fly a kite!"
  Students repeat: "We fly a kite!"
  Teacher says: "He sees a lion!"
  Students repeat: "He sees a lion!"
  Teacher says: "I have a key."
  Students repeat: "I have a key."
  Teacher says: "The lion has a lemon."
  Students repeat: "The lion has a lemon."
