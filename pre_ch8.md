## 1. Letter

### Oo
대문자: O  소문자: o
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽에서 시작, 시계 반대 방향으로 타원
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽에서 시작, 시계 반대 방향으로 작은 타원

### Pp
대문자: P  소문자: p
획순 (대문자): ①→ 위에서 아래 수직선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 곡선 → 수직선 중간까지
획순 (소문자): ①→ 위에서 아래 수직선 (기준선 아래까지) ②→ 오른쪽으로 반원

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /ɒ/ (Oo)
> Open your mouth wide and round your lips slightly.

Sight Words: of / on

Words:
  - octopus 🐙
  - ox 🐂
  - orange 🍊

Sound: /p/ (Pp)
> Press your lips together, then release a small puff of air.

Words:
  - pig 🐷
  - pen ✏️
  - pizza 🍕

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it clearly. Students repeat after you.

Teacher says: "An orange ox."
Students repeat: "An orange ox."

Teacher says: "A pink pig."
Students repeat: "A pink pig."
  Teacher says: "I see an octopus."
  Students repeat: "I see an octopus."
  Teacher says: "The pig has a pen."
  Students repeat: "The pig has a pen."

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /ɒ/ → Students: /ɒ/
Teacher: "ox" → Students: "ox"
Teacher: "orange" → Students: "orange"
Teacher: /p/ → Students: /p/
Teacher: "pig" → Students: "pig"
Teacher: "pizza" → Students: "pizza"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: Is it a / an ___? → Yes, it is.
  - octopus 🐙 → Yes, it is.
  - ox 🐂 → Yes, it is.
  - pig 🐷 → Yes, it is.
  - pen ✏️ → Yes, it is.
