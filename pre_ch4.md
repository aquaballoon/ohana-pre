## 1. Letter

### G  /  g
형태: G (uppercase) — g (lowercase)
획순: ① Start top right → curve counterclockwise in a large arc → ② short horizontal line inward from the right middle
트레이싱: Trace the big G and the small g. Start at the top right.

### H  /  h
형태: H (uppercase) — h (lowercase)
획순: ① Left vertical line down → ② right vertical line down → ③ middle horizontal bar connecting both
트레이싱: Trace the big H and the small h. Start at the top.

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /g/ (G)
> The back of your tongue touches the roof of your mouth. Say /g/ /g/ /g/!

Sight Words: to / I

Words:
  - goat 🐐
  - gift 🎁
  - go 🏃

Sound: /h/ (H)
> Breathe out gently like you're fogging up a mirror. Say /h/ /h/ /h/!

Words:
  - hat 🎩
  - hand ✋
  - hi 👋

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /g/ → Students: /g/
  Teacher: "goat" → Students: "goat"
  Teacher: "gift" → Students: "gift"
  Teacher: /h/ → Students: /h/
  Teacher: "hat" → Students: "hat"
  Teacher: "hand" → Students: "hand"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "Go, goat, go!"
  Students repeat: "Go, goat, go!"
  Teacher says: "Hi! I have a hat."
  Students repeat: "Hi! I have a hat."
  Teacher says: "The goat has a gift."
  Students repeat: "The goat has a gift."
  Teacher says: "A goat and a big hand!"
  Students repeat: "A goat and a big hand!"

[Q&A Pattern]

  - hat 🎩 → It's a hat.
  - hand ✋ → It's a hand.
