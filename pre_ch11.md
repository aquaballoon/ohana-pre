## 1. Letter

### Uu
대문자: U  소문자: u
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽에서 아래로 곡선을 그려 오른쪽 위로 올리기
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽에서 아래 곡선으로 오른쪽 위까지 ②→ 오른쪽에서 아래로 직선

### Vv
대문자: V  소문자: v
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 오른쪽 아래 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위로 사선
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 오른쪽 아래 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위로 사선

### Ww
대문자: W  소문자: w
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 아래 ②→ 가운데 위로 ③→ 다시 아래 ④→ 오른쪽 위로
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 아래 ②→ 가운데 위로 ③→ 다시 아래 ④→ 오른쪽 위로 (작게)

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /ʌ/ (Uu)
> Mouth relaxed and open — "uh!"

Words:
  - umbrella ☂️
  - up 👆

Sound: /v/ (Vv)
> Top teeth on lower lip, voice on — "vvv!"

Words:
  - van 🚐
  - vest 🦺

Sound: /w/ (Ww)
> Round lips, then open — "wuh!"

Words:
  - web 🕸️
  - wolf 🐺

---

Sight Words: with / his / be

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it clearly. Students repeat after you.

Teacher says: "The wolf is in the web!"
Students repeat: "The wolf is in the web!"

Teacher says: "He was with his vest."
Students repeat: "He was with his vest."
  Teacher says: "I have an umbrella and a van."
  Students repeat: "I have an umbrella and a van."
  Teacher says: "I see an umbrella and a web."
  Students repeat: "I see an umbrella and a web."

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /ʌ/ → Students: /ʌ/
Teacher: "umbrella" → Students: "umbrella"
Teacher: "up" → Students: "up"
Teacher: /v/ → Students: /v/
Teacher: "van" → Students: "van"
Teacher: "vest" → Students: "vest"
Teacher: /w/ → Students: /w/
Teacher: "web" → Students: "web"
Teacher: "wolf" → Students: "wolf"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - umbrella ☂️ → It's an umbrella.
  - van 🚐 → It's a van.
  - vest 🦺 → It's a vest.
  - wolf 🐺 → It's a wolf.
