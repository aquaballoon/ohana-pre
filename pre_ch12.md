## 1. Letter

### Xx
대문자: X  소문자: x
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 오른쪽 아래 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 왼쪽 아래 사선 (교차)
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 오른쪽 아래 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 왼쪽 아래 사선 (교차)

### Yy
대문자: Y  소문자: y
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 중앙으로 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 중앙으로 사선 ③→ 중앙에서 아래로 직선
획순 (소문자): ①→ 왼쪽 위에서 오른쪽 아래 사선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 기준선 아래까지 긴 사선

### Zz
대문자: Z  소문자: z
획순 (대문자): ①→ 위 가로선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 왼쪽 아래 사선 ③→ 아래 가로선
획순 (소문자): ①→ 위 짧은 가로선 ②→ 오른쪽 위에서 왼쪽 아래 사선 ③→ 아래 짧은 가로선

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /ks/ (Xx)
> Two sounds together — "k" + "s"!

Words:
  - box 📦
  - x-ray 🩻

Sound: /j/ (Yy)
> Corners of mouth pull back — "yuh!"

Words:
  - yak 🐐
  - yo-yo 🪀

Sound: /z/ (Zz)
> Like /s/ but voice on — "zzz!"

Words:
  - zip 🤐
  - zebra 🦓

---

Sight Words: this / have / from

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it clearly. Students repeat after you.

Teacher says: "The yak has a yo-yo!"
Students repeat: "The yak has a yo-yo!"

Teacher says: "I have this zebra from the zoo."
Students repeat: "I have this zebra from the zoo."
  Teacher says: "I see a box and a zebra."
  Students repeat: "I see a box and a zebra."
  Teacher says: "The zebra has a box and a zip!"
  Students repeat: "The zebra has a box and a zip!"

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /ks/ → Students: /ks/
Teacher: "box" → Students: "box"
Teacher: "x-ray" → Students: "x-ray"
Teacher: /j/ → Students: /j/
Teacher: "yak" → Students: "yak"
Teacher: "yo-yo" → Students: "yo-yo"
Teacher: /z/ → Students: /z/
Teacher: "zip" → Students: "zip"
Teacher: "zebra" → Students: "zebra"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - box 📦 → It's a box.
  - yak 🐐 → It's a yak.
  - yo-yo 🪀 → It's a yo-yo.
  - zebra 🦓 → It's a zebra.
