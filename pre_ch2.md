## 1. Letter

### C  /  c
형태: C (uppercase) — c (lowercase)
획순: ① Start at the top right → curve left in a large arc → stop at the bottom right
트레이싱: Trace the big C and the small c. Start at the top right.

### D  /  d
형태: D (uppercase) — d (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② curve right from the top to the bottom
트레이싱: Trace the big D and the small d. Start at the top.

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /k/ (C)
> The back of your tongue touches the roof of your mouth. Push air out — /k/ /k/ /k/!

Sight Words: is / it

Words:
  - cat 🐱
  - cap 🧢
  - cup 🥤

Sound: /d/ (D)
> Your tongue tip taps just behind your top teeth. Say /d/ /d/ /d/!

Words:
  - dog 🐶
  - duck 🦆
  - door 🚪

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /k/ → Students: /k/
  Teacher: "cat" → Students: "cat"
  Teacher: "cup" → Students: "cup"
  Teacher: /d/ → Students: /d/
  Teacher: "dog" → Students: "dog"
  Teacher: "duck" → Students: "duck"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "a cat / a cap / a cup"
  Students repeat: "a cat / a cap / a cup"
  Teacher says: "the dog / the duck"
  Students repeat: "the dog / the duck"
  Teacher says: "I see a cat and a dog."
  Students repeat: "I see a cat and a dog."
  Teacher says: "The cat has a cap."
  Students repeat: "The cat has a cap."
