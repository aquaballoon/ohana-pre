## 1. Letter

### I  /  i
형태: I (uppercase) — i (lowercase)
획순: ① Top horizontal right → ② vertical line down → ③ bottom horizontal right; lowercase: short vertical line down → dot above
트레이싱: Trace the big I and the small i. Don't forget the dot on i!

### J  /  j
형태: J (uppercase) — j (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② curve left at the bottom like a hook; lowercase: add a dot above
트레이싱: Trace the big J and the small j. Don't forget the dot on j!

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /ɪ/ (I)
> Short, fast sound — the first sound in "it"!

Sight Words: my / you

Words:
  - igloo 🏔️
  - insect 🐛
  - ink 🖊️

Sound: /dʒ/ (J)
> Squeeze your lips together, then burst open — the first sound in "jump"!

Words:
  - jam 🍓
  - jump 🤸
  - jar 🫙

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /ɪ/ → Students: /ɪ/
  Teacher: "igloo" → Students: "igloo"
  Teacher: "insect" → Students: "insect"
  Teacher: /dʒ/ → Students: /dʒ/
  Teacher: "jam" → Students: "jam"
  Teacher: "jar" → Students: "jar"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "Jump! Jump! Jump!"
  Students repeat: "Jump! Jump! Jump!"
  Teacher says: "My jar has jam in it."
  Students repeat: "My jar has jam in it."
  Teacher says: "I see an insect in the igloo."
  Students repeat: "I see an insect in the igloo."
  Teacher says: "I see an igloo and an insect."
  Students repeat: "I see an igloo and an insect."

---
