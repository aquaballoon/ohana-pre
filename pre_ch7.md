## 1. Letter

### Mm
대문자: M  소문자: m
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 세로선 (위→아래)  ②→ 오른쪽 위 대각선 (위 왼쪽→가운데 아래)  ③→ 왼쪽 위 대각선 (가운데 아래→위 오른쪽)  ④→ 오른쪽 세로선 (위→아래)
획순 (소문자): ①→ 세로선 (위→아래)  ②→ 위로 올라가 첫 번째 아치 (→아래)  ③→ 위로 올라가 두 번째 아치 (→아래)

### Nn
대문자: N  소문자: n
획순 (대문자): ①→ 왼쪽 세로선 (위→아래)  ②→ 오른쪽 위 대각선 (위 왼쪽→아래 오른쪽)  ③→ 오른쪽 세로선 (위→아래)
획순 (소문자): ①→ 세로선 (위→아래)  ②→ 위로 올라가 아치 (→아래)

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /m/ (Mm)
> Press your lips together, then open them with a hum. "Mmm."

Sight Words: she / they

Words:
  - map 🗺️
  - moon 🌙
  - mop 🧹

Sound: /n/ (Nn)
> Touch the tip of your tongue to the roof of your mouth. "Nnn."

Words:
  - net 🥅
  - nut 🥜
  - nose 👃

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it clearly. Students repeat after you.

Teacher says: "She has a map."
Students repeat: "She has a map."

Teacher says: "They see the moon."
Students repeat: "They see the moon."
  Teacher says: "I see a net and a nut."
  Students repeat: "I see a net and a nut."
  Teacher says: "A net, a nut, and a map!"
  Students repeat: "A net, a nut, and a map!"

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /m/ → Students: /m/
Teacher: "map" → Students: "map"
Teacher: "moon" → Students: "moon"
Teacher: /n/ → Students: /n/
Teacher: "net" → Students: "net"
Teacher: "nut" → Students: "nut"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: Is it a / an ___? → Yes, it is.
  - map 🗺️ → Yes, it is.
  - moon 🌙 → Yes, it is.
  - net 🥅 → Yes, it is.
  - nut 🥜 → Yes, it is.
