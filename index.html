Why Ohana English?

Learning That Kids Actually Love

No boring textbooks. No passive sit-and-listen. Every lesson is built around activities and games that keep young learners fully engaged.

🎮

Game-Based Activities

Every chapter includes Tracing Game, Memory Game, and Picture Match — interactive challenges built right into the lesson.

🎵

Music & Songs

Children absorb language through rhythm. Every chapter features a curated English song with lyrics, boosting vocabulary retention and pronunciation naturally.

🗣️

Speak from Day One

Echo Practice and Read-Aloud exercises build real speaking confidence. Kids don't just memorize — they use English from the very first class.

🔤

Phonics + Communication

A unique dual-track design teaches the alphabet and phonics alongside real everyday conversations — so learners build both literacy and fluency at once.

🌍

CEFR International Standard

Aligned to CEFR Pre-A1, the globally recognized framework used by schools and institutions worldwide. Your child's progress is measured by a universal benchmark.

🚀

Smooth Bridge to Level 1

Every Pre-Level topic is carefully mapped to Level 1 (CEFR A1) chapters, ensuring a natural, stress-free transition into the next stage of learning.
