## 1. Letter

### A  /  a
형태: A (uppercase) — a (lowercase)
획순: ① left diagonal down → ② right diagonal down → ③ horizontal crossbar
트레이싱: Trace the big A and the small a. Start at the top.

### B  /  b
형태: B (uppercase) — b (lowercase)
획순: ① vertical line down → ② top bump right → ③ bottom bump right
트레이싱: Trace the big B and the small b. Start at the top line.

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /æ/ (A)
> Open your mouth wide. Short, flat sound — like a surprised "ah!"

Sight Words: the / a

Words:
  - ant 🐜
  - apple 🍎
  - alligator 🐊

Sound: /b/ (B)
> Press your lips together, then pop them open with a puff of air.

Words:
  - ball ⚽
  - bag 🎒

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /æ/ → Students: /æ/
  Teacher: "ant" → Students: "ant"
  Teacher: "apple" → Students: "apple"
  Teacher: /b/ → Students: /b/
  Teacher: "ball" → Students: "ball"
  Teacher: "bus" → Students: "bus"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "an ant / an apple / an alligator"
  Students repeat: "an ant / an apple / an alligator"
  Teacher says: "the ball / the bag / the bus"
  Students repeat: "the ball / the bag / the bus"
  Teacher says: "I see an ant and an apple."
  Students repeat: "I see an ant and an apple."
  Teacher says: "I have a ball and a bag."
  Students repeat: "I have a ball and a bag."

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - ant 🐜 → It's an ant.
  - apple 🍎 → It's an apple.
  - ball ⚽ → It's a ball.
  - bag 🎒 → It's a bag.
