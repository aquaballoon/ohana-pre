## 1. Letter

### Ss
대문자: S  소문자: s
획순 (대문자): ①→ 위에서 아래로 S자 곡선 (한 획)
획순 (소문자): ①→ 위에서 아래로 작은 S자 곡선 (한 획)

### Tt
대문자: T  소문자: t
획순 (대문자): ①→ 위 가로선 ②→ 가운데에서 아래로 직선
획순 (소문자): ①→ 위에서 아래로 직선 ②→ 중간 높이에 짧은 가로선

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /s/ (Ss)
> Teeth together, air hisses out — "ssss!"

Words:
  - sun ☀️
  - sock 🧦
  - star ⭐

Sound: /t/ (Tt)
> Tongue taps the top — "t!"

Words:
  - tiger 🐯
  - turtle 🐢
  - train 🚂

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it clearly. Students repeat after you.

  Teacher says: "The sun and the star!"
  Students repeat: "The sun and the star!"
  Teacher says: "A tiger and a train."
  Students repeat: "A tiger and a train."
  Teacher says: "I see a sock and a turtle."
  Students repeat: "I see a sock and a turtle."
  Teacher says: "I see a star and a sun!"
  Students repeat: "I see a star and a sun!"

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /s/ → Students: /s/
Teacher: "sun" → Students: "sun"
Teacher: "sock" → Students: "sock"
Teacher: /t/ → Students: /t/
Teacher: "tiger" → Students: "tiger"
Teacher: "train" → Students: "train"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - sun ☀️ → It's a sun.
  - sock 🧦 → It's a sock.
  - tiger 🐯 → It's a tiger.
  - turtle 🐢 → It's a turtle.
