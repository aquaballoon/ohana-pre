## 1. Letter

### Qq
대문자: Q  소문자: q
획순 (대문자): ①→ 큰 원 그리기 ②→ 오른쪽 아래에 짧은 사선 추가
획순 (소문자): ①→ 작은 원 그리기 ②→ 오른쪽에 아래로 긴 직선

### Rr
대문자: R  소문자: r
획순 (대문자): ①→ 위에서 아래로 직선 ②→ 위 오른쪽 반원 ③→ 오른쪽 아래 사선
획순 (소문자): ①→ 위에서 아래로 직선 ②→ 오른쪽 위로 짧은 곡선

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /kw/ (Qq)
> Lips together, then open with a "w" puff — "kw!"

Words:
  - queen 👑
  - quiet 🤫
  - quilt 🛏️

Sound: /r/ (Rr)
> Tongue curls up slightly, not touching the top — "rrr!"

Words:
  - rainbow 🌈
  - rabbit 🐰
  - rocket 🚀

---

Sight Words: at / up / go

## 4. Speaking

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "The queen is quiet."
  Students repeat: "The queen is quiet."
  Teacher says: "Go up! Look at the rocket!"
  Students repeat: "Go up! Look at the rocket!"
  Teacher says: "I see a rainbow and a rabbit."
  Students repeat: "I see a rainbow and a rabbit."
  Teacher says: "The queen has a quilt."
  Students repeat: "The queen has a quilt."

---

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.

Teacher: /kw/ → Students: /kw/
Teacher: "queen" → Students: "queen"
Teacher: "quiet" → Students: "quiet"
Teacher: /r/ → Students: /r/
Teacher: "rainbow" → Students: "rainbow"
Teacher: "rabbit" → Students: "rabbit"

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - queen 👑 → It's a queen.
  - quilt 🛏️ → It's a quilt.
  - rainbow 🌈 → It's a rainbow.
  - rabbit 🐰 → It's a rabbit.
