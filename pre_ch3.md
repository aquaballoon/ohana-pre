## 1. Letter

### E  /  e
형태: E (uppercase) — e (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② top horizontal right → ③ middle horizontal right → ④ bottom horizontal right
트레이싱: Trace the big E and the small e. Start at the top.

### F  /  f
형태: F (uppercase) — f (lowercase)
획순: ① Vertical line down → ② top horizontal right → ③ middle horizontal right
트레이싱: Trace the big F and the small f. Start at the top.

## 2. Sound & Words

Sound: /e/ (E)
> Open your mouth slightly. Short flat sound — like "eh"!

Sight Words: and / in

Words:
  - egg 🥚
  - elephant 🐘
  - envelope ✉️

Sound: /f/ (F)
> Top teeth on lower lip, blow air out — /f/ /f/ /f/!

Words:
  - fish 🐟
  - frog 🐸
  - fan 🌬️

## 4. Speaking

[Echo Practice]
Teacher Instruction: Say each sound clearly. Students echo back immediately.
  Teacher: /e/ → Students: /e/
  Teacher: "egg" → Students: "egg"
  Teacher: "elephant" → Students: "elephant"
  Teacher: /f/ → Students: /f/
  Teacher: "fish" → Students: "fish"
  Teacher: "frog" → Students: "frog"

[Read-Aloud]
Teacher Instruction: Point to each word and say it. Students repeat after you.
  Teacher says: "an egg / an elephant / in the egg"
  Students repeat: "an egg / an elephant / in the egg"
  Teacher says: "a fish / a frog / a fish and a frog"
  Students repeat: "a fish / a frog / a fish and a frog"
  Teacher says: "I see an egg and a fish."
  Students repeat: "I see an egg and a fish."
  Teacher says: "The elephant and the frog."
  Students repeat: "The elephant and the frog."

---

[Q&A Pattern]
Pattern: What's this? → It's a / an ___.
  - egg 🥚 → It's an egg.
  - elephant 🐘 → It's an elephant.
  - fish 🐟 → It's a fish.
  - frog 🐸 → It's a frog.
